About

Zachary Neumann graduated Portland State University with a B.S. in Environmental Science and Management and a minor in Geographic Information Systems. Currently a solutions engineer for the United States Forest Service, he configures and develops Talking Points Collaborative Mapping, the agencies main public participatory GIS application. Zachary seeks to help bridge the communication gap between people and government to bring a voice to those who have not been heard.

Solutions Architect - Front End Development - Python - GIS Analyst - ArcGIS API for Python - ArcPy - ArcGIS Online
